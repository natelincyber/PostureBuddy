What is Posture Buddy?

We noticed that many members in our community fail to speak up about issues that affect them everyday, either due to fear or a lack of knowledge about proper presentation etiquette. With Posture Buddy, these individuals can focus on creating a perfect proposal without having to worry about the formalities. Our algorithm detects many common forms of bad posture and shows aspiring presenters how to fix them in a easy to understand format. Click the button to begin!
